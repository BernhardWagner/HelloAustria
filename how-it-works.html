Welcome to Austria!

We all have been there at some point: you are so excited to ﬁnally be travelling to a new country and try your best to ﬁnd the true must do’s there. You spend hours and hours doing research and in the end, you end up with the same cliché, very touristy attractions. How about a ride on Vienna’s Riesenrad, visiting the famous Schloss Schönbrunn, oh and don’t forget a day trip to Salzburg to see Mozart’s home! While all of these things are special for „Austria Newbies“ , you might be the one to want more of your trip. You want to see the hidden surprises, the unique and magical places in Austria most people don’t visit. If that’s the case, welcome to Österreich!

You can ﬁnd nine icons on the map. Each icon represents something special about each state in Austria. You can click on each one of them to magically appear in those places. All you then need to do is explore.

Now you are visiting one of many spots in Austria. Each place is divided into 3 layers. Each layer has 2-3 hidden surprises, so make sure to ﬁnd them!

  • To close the window and return to the map, click on the X in the top right corner.
  • To get more information about this place, click on the i in the bottom right corner.

To get to the next layer, simply double click. Easy peasy, now you can enjoy the second and third layer!

After having explored all places, how about sharing this guide with all of your wonderful fellow travellers?
